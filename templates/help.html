INTRODUCTION

In today's fast-paced learning environment, engaging users effectively is essential. Our Quiz Application not only makes learning enjoyable but also effective, ensuring users gain and retain knowledge in a modern, interactive format.

ACCOUNT CREATION

  1. Visit the Profile Section on the Homepage
    • Go to the profile section of the quiz-making application.
  2. Click on "Create Account"
    • Look for the “Create Account” button, usually located at the profile section of the page.
  3. Fill Out the Registration Form
    • Username: Choose a unique username.
    • Firstname: Enter your first name.
    • lastname: Enter your last name.
    • Email Address: Enter a valid email address.
    • Password: Create a secure password (minimum 8 characters recommended).
    • Confirm Password: Re-enter your password for confirmation.
  4. Click "Submit"
    • After filling out the form, click the “Submit” button.

LOGGING IN

  1. Visit the Profile Section on the Homepage
    • Go to the profile section of the quiz-making application.
  2. Click on "Sign In”
    • Look for the “Sign In” button, usually located at the profile section of the page.
  3. Fill Out the Details required
    • Username: enter your username
    • Password: enter a secure password (minimum 8 characters recommended).
  4. Click "Submit"
    • After filling out the form, click the “Submit” button.
