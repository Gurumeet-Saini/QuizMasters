THE ART OF STORYTELLING

WHAT TO DO

• Be original - write your own stories, from your imagination or real experiences.

• Be creative - use your words to build exciting, funny, emotional, or inspiring stories.

• Be respectful - write stories that are kind and welcoming to all readers.

• Check spelling and grammar - it helps everyone understand your story better.

WHAT NOT TO DO

• No hate speech - avoid language that disrespects or hurts others.

• No bullying, threats, or violence - stories should create a safe space.

• No inappropriate or adult content - keep your story suitable for all age groups.

• No copying others' work - write your own story, don't copy-paste from websites or books.
