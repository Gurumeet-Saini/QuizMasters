BIG IDEAS IN SMALL SNAPS

GUIDELINES

• Video Quality: Upload videos in 720p or higher resolution, MP4/MOV format, under 500 MB, with clear audio.

• Video Length: Keep videos between 1 to 2 minutes for quick, engaging content.

• Content Guidelines: Provide educational content, use a positive tone, and ensure clear explanations.

• Title & Description: Add a descriptive title and a brief description (2-3 sentences) explaining the content.

• Thumbnail: Use an eye-catching thumbnail (1280x720 pixels) for better engagement.

• Copyright & Ownership: Ensure videos are original or you have permission for any copyrighted material, with proper attribution.
